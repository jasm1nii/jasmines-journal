the webmaster

my background

like many people on neocities, i'm entirely self-taught in HTML and CSS. i first dipped my toes in front-end design as an elementary student toying with multiply blog layouts (back when i was definitely too young to be on the internet). sadly, i abandoned my humble insite text editor when the service shut down and didn't code very much for years, as the newer, more popular "social" networks didn't allow for customization to that extent (save for tumblr, which never felt quite as cozy).

it wasn't until i caught wind of sites like cohost, spacehey, and neocities that i was incentivized to brush up on my "lost skills". i needed alternative spaces where i could be more genuine with less pressure to censor my thoughts, so here i am!

while i don't have a formal education in web development, my sense of layouting and aesthetics is largely influenced by my undergraduate studies in visual communication design, where i also concentrated in multimedia. but even so, i'm much more used to outputting non-interactive work with programs like clip studio paint, illustrator, indesign, and after effects - hopefully, i'll show off more of my creative projects as this site comes together! ✧
