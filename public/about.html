the webmaster

my background

like many people on neocities, i'm entirely self-taught in HTML and CSS. i first dipped my toes in front-end design as an elementary student toying with multiply blog layouts (back when i was definitely too young to be on the internet). sadly, i abandoned my humble insite text editor when the service shut down and didn't code for years afterwards, as the newer, more popular "social" networks didn't allow for customization to that extent.*

* except for tumblr, but it never felt "cozy" enough some reason...

it wasn't until i caught wind of sites like cohost, spacehey, and neocities that i was incentivized to brush up on my "lost skills". i needed alternative spaces where i could be more genuine with less pressure to censor my thoughts... and here i am!

while i don't have a formal education in web development, my sense of layouting and aesthetics is largely influenced by my undergraduate background in visual communication design with a concentration in multimedia. by experience, i'm much more accustomed to outputting non-interactive visual work, such as illustrations, graphic designs, and motion art - so i hope to eventually show off my full range of skills as this site comes together! ✧
