the website

what's this?

welcome to my digital sandbox! the creation of this website in july 2023 was motivated by a personal desire to reclaim my online presence and explore a digital self-expression medium with fewer restraints, beyond what corporate social media and other content hosts are currently capable of offering.

it is coded entirely by hand without the use of conventional site builder tools - an initially daunting task as someone without any serious web development experience beforehand. but i'm proud to say that i've learned a lot since embarking on this journey!

with that in mind, i fully intend to use this space to showcase whatever i want, however i want - whether that be personal interests, my thoughts on various subjects, or stuff i've worked on.

the webmaster

my background

like many people on the indie web, i'm entirely self-taught in HTML and CSS. i first dipped my toes in front-end design as an elementary student toying with multiply blog layouts (back when i was definitely too young to be on the internet). sadly, i abandoned my humble insite text editor when the service shut down and didn't code for years afterwards, as the newer, more popular "social" networks didn't allow for customization to that extent, save for tumblr.

it wasn't until i caught wind of sites like cohost, spacehey, and neocities that i was incentivized to brush up on my "lost skills". i needed alternative spaces where i could be more genuine with less pressure to conform to the norms of commercial social networks... so, here i am! ‡´Æ À∂·µî ·µï ·µîÀ∂ ·Éê
